Venue Hire

Here at Multi-Sound Venue we try to offer our clients and customers as much diversity as possible. Like with our venue, we offer for hire, for many different types of performances and at very competitive prices. Our venue is available for hire for all types of concert style events, whether it be for local up and coming talent pushing the launch of their new album to your more established artists. Our 550 person capacity concert hall will be ideal for your big night. We also offer our venue to hire for theatre style events. Our hall can be transformed into a 150 seater theatre venue for all sorts shows, from well established drama and plays to your more festive pantomime style shows, there is something for all the arts here at Multi-Sound. We also like to give back to the community with our very special rates for local clubs, societies and colleges to hire out the venue. For any more information on our venue or our rates just contact us.

Nightclub Events

Diversity being our business here at Multi-Sound Venue, we like to offer more than just concerts and theatre. We also have a nightclub at hand for our customers to enjoy. If you are feeling like the show and party is not after post performance, you will not have to go far to find your next stop. At Multi-Sound nightclub we have a late bar 7 days a week so for us and you the night does not end when the curtains are drawn. Those customers wishing to avail of our nightclub facilities after the show, just produce your ticket for the nights event and gain free entry.

Nightclub Hire

We also offer our customers the opportunity to hire our or Nightclub out for parties and events. We offer very competitive rates on both out Nightclub and drink specials to those who choose to hold their event at Multi-Sound Nightclub. If you choose to avail of our top hire package we at Multi-Sound can supply you with one of our professional DJs for your event or if you would prefer we would be more than happy to let you provide your own. If you choose Multi-Sound Nightclub, we will provide all the bar-staff, waiters, security and products for your event.

Box Office

Tickets for our theatre and concert shows will be available at all your usual on-line and in store ticket agents, such as Ticketmaster. But for those shows which do not sell out, we do have an on-site box office at Multi-Sound venue where you can both pre-book your tickets for collection and also buy all available tickets on the day of the show at our box office.

Career Opportunities

At Multi-Sound venue we offer a lot of opportunities for employment and careers, from bar staff and waiters to sound and light engineers. There is plenty on offer for the educated and experienced and for those without. We also look for many acting roles in up coming shows and do what we can to put your name forward to all openings in such areas. We also offer lots of intern positions for students in the are to work and shadow in many different aspects of the music and theatre industries.

Disabled Services

At Multi-Sound we are very proud of our work and efforts to make or establishment a better experience and place to visit for those with disabilities in out society. We offer top of the range disability facilities such as ramps in, out and around the venue to make access easier. We have top state of the art disabled restroom facilities. We have special areas for our disabled customers to view the shows from. We also have constant on-site personal to help our disabled customers with any of their or to just help transport in and around the building and to your specified areas.

FAQ

What day and time is the box office open?
Box office is open on all event days from 6pm to 9pm.

How do apply I for a position at Multi-Sound?
Email us at careers@multisound.ie

Are their cloak room Facilities?
Yes, you can leave your cloaks and other personal possessions, in the cloak room just inside the front door.

Are their any age restrictions into Multi-Sound?
Some shows have age restrictions, see ticket for details.

Can I bring in outside food and drink?
We would appreciate you not bringing in outside food and drink as their are such services provided in the venue.

Can I bring a camera to the show?
We allow photographic cameras to be brought to the show, for you to enjoy memories of your night. But we would appreciate if you would refrain from video recording our professional shows. Our club, society and college shows can be recorded unless specifically stated against.
