Your Music, Your Way

Welcome to Dublin's newest and best music venue. Located in the heart of the city, we cater for everybody who wants to be on stage! From new and unsigned bands, to established acts.


Pop in any night and you're bound to find great music, amazing theatre performances or just a good old nightclub session! We have a fully stocked bar ready to be demolished! Just check out the upcoming shows for more info.


If you are a band, theatre group or promoter looking for an exciting new venue to showcase your talents, then fill out our booking form now, we promise that we can fulfill all of your needs and desires.
